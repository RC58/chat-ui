  • Features

A Learner gets access to host of value added features when he registers with Eduauraa.

EPQ

EPQ (Eduauraa Proficiency Quotient) provides required reports and study analytics to enable a learner to evaluate its learning milestones. AI-based evaluation is used to map the learning curve of each learner. Quick learning solutions are provided to improve weak areas.

By combining all various input aspects of a user, AI based EPQ plays a key role in analysing and interpreting vast amounts of learner data. We use AI technique to check the sequence of responses to determine learning pattern, progress growth, and weak areas to provide instant important user analytics.

EA

With Eduauraa Assistant a learner is able to plan and schedule his learning requirements. EA works as an assistant to schedule learning milestones. Learner can schedule study of a learning video for a future date. EA helps a learner to choose a desired date and time for the study. Learner will get required notifications and alerts as per the schedule of learning hence pushing him to study as per his plan. Learner can also schedule learning milestones as per the study goals.

My Downloads

A learner can download videos for future learning in an Offline Mode. Eduauraa State of Art mobile APP technology allows a learner to download a learning video within the App for future study. Downloaded video can be played anytime even if there is no internet connectivity.

Doubt Solver

For any doubts, learner can submit their queries to APP assistant and get quick solution from the Eduauraa experts.

Virtual Class

Virtual Classroom enables learning with peers within a Virtual Environment. This means students can study together visually by watching videos at the same time, or giving tests or quizzes against each other. There is even room for peer to peer assessment if a child is comfortable with that. Students can invite your friends and peers to study together in the Virtual classroom.

Mentor

At the point when a student needs some assistance to clarify a concept, at that point Eduauraa provides the right Mentor for one-to-one study. A learner can book live online session with his choice of Mentor according to the calendar and mentor availability. Various availabilities and adaptable dates are accessible to choose from. This takes out the need to travel and gives extra security to needy students who now and then face predicament while going for a tuition class. Our Mentor feature enables a student to study with a prestigious educator from the advantage of his home.
